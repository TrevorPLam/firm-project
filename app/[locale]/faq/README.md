# FAQ Page

Frequently asked questions page.

## Purpose

Provides answers to common questions about services, process, pricing, and company information.

## Route

- **Path**: `/faq` (localized: `/en/faq`, `/es/faq`)
- **Type**: Server component with static rendering

## Content Sections

- **Hero** - Page title and introduction
- **FAQ Categories** - Organized questions by topic:
  - General
  - Web Design & Development
  - SEO Services
  - Analytics & Reporting
  - Pricing & Process
- **Contact CTA** - Call-to-action for further questions

## FAQ Categories

### General
- Services offered
- Business history
- Client sizes served
- Industry specializations

### Web Design & Development
- Technologies used
- Custom vs template designs
- Mobile responsiveness
- Maintenance support
- Timeline estimates

### SEO Services
- Result timelines
- Ranking guarantees
- Service inclusions
- Local SEO

### Analytics & Reporting
- Tools used (GA4, GTM)
- Reporting frequency
- Data interpretation
- Conversion tracking

### Pricing & Process
- Pricing structure
- Payment terms
- Project process
- Ownership rights

## Features

- Breadcrumb structured data (schema.org)
- FAQ structured data (schema.org)
- Scroll reveal animations
- Internationalization support
- SEO metadata with locale alternates

## Notes

FAQ content is currently hardcoded in the component. Future versions may move to CMS for easier updates.
