# Terms of Service Page

Terms of service legal documentation.

## Purpose

Outlines the terms and conditions for using the website and services provided.

## Route

- **Path**: `/legal/terms` (localized: `/en/legal/terms`, `/es/legal/terms`)
- **Type**: Server component with static rendering

## Content Sections

- Agreement to Terms
- Services Description
- Client Responsibilities
- Payment Terms
- Intellectual Property
- Confidentiality
- Limitation of Liability
- Termination
- Governing Law
- Changes to Terms
- Contact Information

## Features

- Internationalization support
- SEO metadata with locale alternates
- Scroll-based reveal animations
- Legal document formatting

## Notes

This document should be reviewed by legal counsel to ensure it adequately protects your business interests and complies with applicable laws.
