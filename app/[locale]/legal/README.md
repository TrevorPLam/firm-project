# Legal Pages

Legal documentation pages for privacy policy and terms of service.

## Purpose

Provides legal documentation required for website operation and user protection.

## Structure

- `privacy/` - Privacy policy page
- `terms/` - Terms of service page

## Routes

- **Privacy**: `/legal/privacy` (localized)
- **Terms**: `/legal/terms` (localized)
- **Type**: Server components with static rendering

## Privacy Policy

Covers data collection, usage, storage, user rights, and contact information for privacy inquiries.

## Terms of Service

Covers service terms, user responsibilities, limitations of liability, and dispute resolution.

## Features

- Internationalization support
- SEO metadata with locale alternates
- Scroll-based reveal animations
- Legal document formatting

## Notes

Legal content should be reviewed by legal counsel. These templates provide a starting point but may need customization based on specific business requirements and jurisdictions.
