# Privacy Policy Page

Privacy policy legal documentation.

## Purpose

Discloses data collection, usage, and protection practices to comply with privacy regulations (GDPR, CCPA, etc.).

## Route

- **Path**: `/legal/privacy` (localized: `/en/legal/privacy`, `/es/legal/privacy`)
- **Type**: Server component with static rendering

## Content Sections

- Introduction
- Information We Collect (data collection practices)
- How We Use Your Information (data usage)
- Information Sharing (data sharing policies)
- Data Security (security measures)
- Your Rights (user rights and choices)
- Cookies (tracking technologies)
- Contact Us (contact information for privacy inquiries)

## Features

- Internationalization support
- SEO metadata with locale alternates
- Scroll-based reveal animations
- Legal document formatting

## Notes

This document should be reviewed by legal counsel to ensure compliance with applicable privacy laws in your jurisdiction.
